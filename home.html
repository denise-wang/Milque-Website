Milque is a collective founded on inclusivity, artistic freedom, and friendship.

A collaborative community - by artists for artists.

Talent is everywhere, yet only well funded artists have access to proper studios and capable collaborators. Expensive studio rates and a limited network leaves most artists with unprofessional products and an incomplete suite of content. The Campus is a place for artists to grow, rather than where they simply create, The Campus forms a diversified artistic community in which members collaborate towards the common goal of making worthwhile art.
